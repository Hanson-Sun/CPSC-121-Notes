Evaluation Rules

  1. Commutative Law: P ∧ Q ≡ Q ∧ P
  2. Associative Law: P ∨ (Q∨R) ≡ (P∨Q) ∨ R [MUST BE SAME OPERATOR]
  3. Distributive Law: P ∧ (Q∨R) ≡ (P∧Q) ∨ (P∧R)
  4. Identity Laws: P ∧ t ≡ P   and   P ∨ c ≡ P
  5. Negation Laws: P ∨ ¬P ≡ t   and   P ∧ ¬P ≡ c
  6. Double Negative Law: ¬(¬P) ≡ P
  7. Idempotent Law: P ∧ P ≡ P   and   P ∨ P ≡ P
  8. Universal Bound Laws: P ∨ t ≡ t   and   P ∧ c ≡ c
  9. De Morgan’s Laws: ¬(P∧Q) ≡ ¬P ∨ ¬Q   and   ¬(P∨Q) ≡ ¬P ∧ ¬Q
  10. Absorption Laws: P ∨ (P∧Q) ≡ P   and   P ∧ (P∨Q) ≡ P
  11. Negations of t and c: ¬t ≡ c   and   ¬c ≡ t
  12. Idempotent Laws (NAND and NOR): ¬P ≡ P|P   similarly,  ¬P ≡ P ↓ P
  13. Conditionals

Order of Operations

  1. ¬; Negations first
  2. ∧,∨; Evaluate ∧ and ∨ second. If both are present () may be needed
  3. ⟹,⇔; Evaluate ⟹ and ⇔ third. If both are present () may be needed
